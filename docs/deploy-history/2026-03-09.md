- `frontend/src/lib/query-provider.tsx`: MutationCache `onError`에 `silent` meta 지원
- `frontend/src/hooks/use-dashboard.ts`: useKisStatus 타입에 `token_valid` 추가
- `frontend/src/hooks/use-holdings-mutations.ts`: `useSilentSyncHoldings` 훅 추가
